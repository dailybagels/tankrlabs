About

Tankr Labs is an aggregator focused on one thing: better smart contract audits for crypto teams.

Our role

We’re the connector and coordinator — helping founders find the right auditor quickly, and helping auditors focus on the work (not admin).

Why it works

Clean scopes, ready repos, comparable proposals, and clear expectations reduce friction for both sides.

What we don’t do

  • No in‑house triage or IR
  • No exploit‑as‑a‑service
  • No bounty management
  • No threat intel subscriptions

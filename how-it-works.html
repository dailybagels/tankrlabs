How it works

A simple, founder‑friendly flow to get the right audit.

1) Submit your request

Share repo access (read‑only), desired timelines, contracts in scope, test coverage, and constraints.

2) We curate matches

We shortlist auditors with relevant domain expertise, availability, and track record.

3) Compare proposals

Receive 2–4 proposals with scope, methods, deliverables, team, duration, and pricing.

4) Kickoff & delivery

We coordinate kickoff, cadence, and deliverable format. You liaise primarily with your chosen auditor.

Deliverables you can expect

  • Prioritized findings (with severity)
  • Clear repro steps or PoCs where applicable
  • Remediation guidance
  • Re‑test report (optional)
